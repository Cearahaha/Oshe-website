The Team

The people behind
OSHE and HARM.

A small, experienced team with deep roots in New Zealand health and safety. We built the tools we wished existed, and now we help other organisations use them properly.

Co-Founders
Vanessa Crafts
Co-Founder · OSHE and HARM

Vanessa has over 10 years in health and safety working with New Zealand businesses across trades, construction, social services, and beyond. She built HARM to give organisations access to straight, practical H&S advice without the jargon, and co-founded OSHE to put the right tools in the hands of the people actually doing the work. She leads HARM consulting personally and is available for 1:1 sessions with business owners who want honest, expert eyes on their setup.

Sophie Donnelly
Co-Founder · OSHE and HARM

Sophie is more interested in clarity than box-ticking. Her focus is on people-first safety that actually works in practice, not just on paper. She reads a room fast, earns trust quickly, and has a talent for cutting through complexity to find what organisations actually need to change. She brings the operational backbone to both OSHE and HARM.
