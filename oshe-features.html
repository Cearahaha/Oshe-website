01
Contractor Management

Smart onboarding and a register that builds itself.

Getting a new contractor on site used to mean chasing paperwork for weeks. OSHE automates the whole process. Pre-qualification applications go out automatically, come back into the system, and get assigned to the right person for review. Insurances and certifications are tracked, and the right people get notified before anything lapses.

  • Automated pre-qualification applications sent and returned
  • All documents stored and organised per contractor
  • Insurance and certification renewal alerts
  • Owner assigned per contractor for accountability
  • Full contractor register built automatically
02
QR Sign In and Out

No app needed for site sign-in. Just a phone camera.

Post a QR code at the site entrance. Workers and visitors scan it with their phone camera, sign in, and complete their induction in the same flow. No app download, no account creation, no friction for people coming on site. You get instant visibility of exactly who is there at any given moment.

  • Works with any smartphone camera, no app download needed for sign-in
  • Induction completed in the same scan-in flow
  • Sign in and out tracked in real time
  • Visitor log visible to all internal users
  • Sign-out prompts workers to flag any incidents
03
Job Management and SSP

Site Safety Plans in minutes, not hours.

SSPs usually take a couple of hours to build from scratch. OSHE pre-loads your risk assessments, links your existing registers, and uses a guided questionnaire to build the document for you. It stays live for the full life of the contract, with every incident report, risk assessment, and completed form automatically added to the record.

  • Guided questionnaire builds the SSP for you
  • Pre-loaded risk assessments and templates
  • Registers linked and pulled in automatically
  • All incidents and forms consolidated in one place
  • Full job history retained for compliance
  • Accessible from the mobile app at all times
04
Incident Reporting

Report at sign-out. Notify instantly.

When workers sign out for the day, they are prompted to flag any incidents. The right people get notified automatically. Corrective actions can be assigned and tracked right through to closure. Privacy controls mean only the people who need to see it can see it. Our customers have seen an 80% increase in incident reporting since switching to OSHE.

  • Incident flagging built into the sign-out flow
  • Automatic notifications to the right people
  • Corrective actions assigned and tracked to closure
  • Privacy controlled throughout
  • Reportable from the desktop too
05
Inductions and Training

Baseline modules included. Fully customisable on top.

Most organisations hand someone a form on day one and call it an induction. OSHE includes ready-to-go baseline induction modules so people actually understand what incident reporting, hazard management, and your H&S policy mean in practice. Add your own modules on top. Two-step sign-off keeps everything accountable.

  • Baseline modules included from day one
  • Add your own custom induction modules
  • Completed through the QR sign-in flow on site
  • Two-step sign-off by worker and manager
  • Training register with expiry alerts per certificate
  • Certificates stored and accessible by each user
06
Hazard and Safety Registers

All your registers. Linked, tracked, and always up to date.

OSHE holds four linked registers: hazard, PPE, plant and equipment, and hazardous substances. Every change is tracked with who made it and when, giving you a full compliance history. The hazardous substances register is accessible on mobile, which means no more folders of data sheets in every vehicle. All registers download as print-ready documents.

  • Hazard, PPE, plant and equipment, and hazardous substances
  • Pre-loaded hazard library to get started fast
  • Full change history tracked for compliance
  • Hazardous substance data sheets accessible on mobile
  • Notes and comments against any hazard record
  • Download as print-ready documents
07
Smart Notifications

Set it. Forget it. Stay compliant without the mental load.

Keeping track of what is expiring and when is one of the biggest admin headaches in H&S. OSHE tracks every expiry across your whole system and notifies the right person before things lapse. Insurance renewals, training due dates, substance data sheet updates, contractor re-prequalifications. Nothing falls through the cracks.

  • Insurance and certification renewal reminders
  • Training and induction expiry alerts
  • Contractor re-prequalification prompts
  • Hazardous substance data sheet update reminders
  • All notifications assigned to the right person
08
File and Document Hub

One place for all your H&S documents.

Stop emailing PDFs around or hunting through shared drives. The OSHE file hub lets you upload policies, procedures, toolbox talks, and any other documents into organised folders. Accessible from any device, instantly shared across your whole team.

  • Organised folder structure you control
  • Accessible from desktop and mobile
  • Any file type supported
  • Shared across your whole team instantly
  • 5GB storage on Starter, 20GB on Professional
09
Mobile App

Works offline. Built for site, not the office.

The OSHE mobile app works without internet. Fill in forms, log hazards, complete task analyses, report incidents. Everything syncs automatically when you get back in range. The desktop handles back-end management and reporting. The mobile app handles everything on site.

  • Full offline capability on the mobile app
  • Auto-sync when back online
  • Incident reporting, hazard logging, and forms all available offline
  • Desktop handles back-end management and reporting
  • Available on any smartphone via the browser
